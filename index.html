Macaroni and cheese omelette

Ingredient Amount
uncooked macaroni 100 g.
beaten egg 5 pc.
grated cheese 150 g.
chopped basil leaf 2 tbsp.
olive oil 1 tbsp.

Order of preparation:

  1. Heat the oven to 180 degrees
  2. Cook macaroni in boiling salted water for 12 min.
  3. Drain carefully and let cool slightly
  4. Mix in the beaten eggs, half the cheese and chopped basil and season with salt and pepper
  5. Preheat the grill to maximum heat
  6. Heat the oil in an ovenproof frying pan, moving the pan so that the oil covers the sides. Then pour the omelet mixture into the pan and smooth the surface
  7. Bake on low for 4 minutes and sprinkle with remaining cheese.
  8. Place the pan under the grill and cook until the top is golden
  9. Serve as is or top with grilled bacon
